I am a Ph.D. student in Design at North Carolina State University, with research interests in building energy systems, grid-interactive buildings, and data-driven energy analytics. I am also pursuing graduate study in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My research focuses on data-driven methods for understanding how buildings and electric grids interact under normal, extreme, and recovery conditions. Current directions include building–grid resilience, power outage forecasting, heat pump identification, and thermal-aware operation of high-performance computing environments.

<hr style="border:0; border-top:2px solid #dddddd; width:80px; margin:1.1em 0;">

Research Interests
======

- Building energy systems and grid-interactive buildings
- Building-grid resilience, outage forecasting, and restoration dynamics
- Heat pump identification using AMI and weather data (*NLR*)
- Thermal-aware computing and data center energy management
- Machine learning and time-series analytics for energy systems
